How to Use This Website

Welcome to Conestoga Scheduling Page. Please follow the steps below to get started:

  • Navigation: Use the navigation bar at the top to browse main sections.
  • Course Explorer: Click on The Program of Studies to view our curriculum catalog and explore learning opportunities.
  • Select: Click on a Grade to view selections possible for the grade. Make sure to meet all graduation Requirments.
  • Review: Click on View Schedule page to view your full selection.
  • Export: If everything looks good, click Export to export all selections into powerschool.

If you encounter any issues, please ask your counselor.

Graduation Requirements

To graduate, students must successfully complete a minimum of 24 total credits, including the following specific credit requirements:

  • English: 4 Credits
  • Math: 3 Credits
  • Science: 3 Credits
  • Social Studies: 3 Credits
  • World Language: 2 Credits
  • Electives: 4.5 Credits

In addition to the credits above, your schedule must also include at least one course from each of the following categories:

  • Business or Technology (BU/TE/BT)
  • Family and Consumer Sciences (FC)
  • Visual & Performing Arts (VP)
  • College Career Transition (CT)

Promotion Requirements

  • Grade 9 to 10: Students must have passed at least four major courses and earned at least 5.5 credits.
  • Grade 10 to 11: Students must have earned at least 11.0 credits.
  • Grade 11 to 12: Students must have earned at least 17.0 credits. However, final decisions on promotion to grade 12 may be based on whether the student can be scheduled during the regular school day for the courses needed for a June graduation.
