How to Use This Website

Welcome to Conestoga Scheuduling. Please follow the steps below to get started:

  • Navigation: Use the navigation bar at the top to browse main sections (loaded via nav.js).
  • Course Explorer: Click on "Courses" (if available in the navbar) to view our curriculum catalog and explore learning opportunities (loaded via courses.js).
  • Search: Use any available search tools to find specific courses or district content.
  • Account: Log in using your credentials (if applicable) for personalized content and tools.

If you encounter any issues, please check our FAQ section or contact IT support.
