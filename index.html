How to Use This Website

Welcome to Conestoga Scheduling. Please follow the steps below to get started:

  • Navigation: Use the navigation bar at the top to browse main sections (loaded via nav.js).
  • Course Explorer: Click on "Courses" (if available in the navbar) to view our curriculum catalog and explore learning opportunities (loaded via courses.js).
  • Search: Use any available search tools to find specific courses or district content.
  • Account: Log in using your credentials (if applicable) for personalized content and tools.

If you encounter any issues, please check our FAQ section or contact IT support.

Graduation Requirements

To graduate, students must successfully complete a minimum of 24 total credits, including the following specific credit requirements:

  • English: 4 Credits
  • Math: 3 Credits
  • Science: 3 Credits
  • Social Studies: 3 Credits
  • World Language: 2 Credits
  • Electives: 4.5 Credits

In addition to the credits above, your schedule must also include at least one course from each of the following categories:

  • Business or Technology (BU/TE/BT)
  • Family and Consumer Sciences (FC)
  • Visual & Performing Arts (VP)
  • College Career Transition (CT)
