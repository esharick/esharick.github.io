How to get your watch-history.html file

To get your watch history, you need to download it from Google Takeout. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Go to Google Takeout
  2. Sign in with your Google account if you haven't already
  3. Click on "Deselect all"
  4. Find and select "YouTube and YouTube Music"
  5. Click on "Next step"
  6. Choose the delivery you can access
  7. Click on "Create export"
  8. Wait for the export to be created
  9. Download the archive
  10. Extract the archive and find the "watch-history.html" file
