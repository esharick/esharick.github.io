Our Story

At Uncle Jack & Son LLC, we're more than just a weapons dealer—we're a family-owned business with a passion for firearms. Founded in 2002 by Jack and his son, our journey began with a love for quality craftsmanship and a commitment to customer satisfaction. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, we've become a trusted name in firearms and weaponry. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse selection of handguns, rifles, shotguns, and tactical gear, all carefully curated to meet the needs of gun enthusiasts, hunters, and law enforcement agencies. But our dedication doesn't stop there. We're also strong advocates for responsible gun ownership and safety education. Through community outreach programs and partnerships with local organizations, we strive to promote firearm safety and training. At Uncle Jack & Son LLC, our customers always come first. Our knowledgeable team is here to provide personalized service and expert guidance, ensuring you find the perfect firearm for your needs. Thank you for choosing Uncle Jack & Son LLC as your trusted firearms partner. We look forward to serving you for many years to come.
